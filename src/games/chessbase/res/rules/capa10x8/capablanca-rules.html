Differences from orthodox chess

Played on a 10x8 board, 2 new pieces are involved:

  • The Chancellor: moves and captures like Knight + Rook

  • The Archbishop: moves and captures like Knight + Bishop

Castling :

  • Capablanca, Gothic, Bird : the king moves three squares
  • Carrera : no castling
  • Embassy : the king moves three spaces toward the Rook
  • Ladorean : the king moves two spaces toward the Rook
  • Janus : the king moves close to the rook initial position
  • Schoolbook, Univers, Grotesque : the king two or more spaces toward the Rook
